Beginner level- Single dice


  • - The game has 2 players, playing in rounds.
  • - In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as they whishe. Each result get added to their ROUND score.
  • - BUT, if the player rolls a 1, all their ROUND score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn.
  • - The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that their ROUND score gets added to his GLBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn.
  • - The first player to reach 100 points on GLOBAL score wins the game.



Pro level- Double dice


  • -Manually set your decided game winning score.
  • - The game has 2 players, playing in rounds.
  • - In each turn, a player rolls the 2 dice as many times as they whish. Each result get added to their ROUND score.
  • -BUT, if the player rolls 2 1s or 6s in on their dice, all their ROUND score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn.
  • - The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that their ROUND score gets added to their GLBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn.
  • - The first player to reach the set winning points on GLOBAL score wins the game!
